About

Lumi Cake Baseboard Company

At Lumi Cake Baseboard Company, we provide high-quality, durable, and beautifully crafted cake baseboards for both professional and home bakers. Using premium, food-safe materials, our boards are designed to be sturdy and visually appealing, supporting various cake types, from single-layer treats to multi-tiered masterpieces.

Our products include classic round and square boards, luxury cake drums, eco-friendly options, and custom-shaped designs. We provide multiple colors, finishes, and thicknesses to meet the needs of various events, from birthdays and weddings to themed events and high-end celebrations. With this diverse selection, we offer the perfect solution for professional and home bakers alike.
